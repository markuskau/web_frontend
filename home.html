App info

This wellness app helps you track two important aspects of your health: body mass index (BMI) and sleep quality. The app aims to provide a clear and easy-to-use way to understand your own well-being and support a healthy lifestyle.

The app allows you to calculate and track your BMI by entering your height and weight. BMI helps you assess whether your weight is at a healthy level for your height. The app saves your previous measurements, allowing you to view changes over time and track your progress.

By combining BMI and sleep tracking, the app provides a more holistic view of your well-being. Clear summaries and tracking help you make more informed decisions about your health and everyday life. The app is designed to support small but meaningful changes towards better well-being.
